About Us at Boerejongens

Boerejongens (slang word for proper "BoereNjongens") means "Farmer Boys" in Dutch.
Pronounce it like boor-a-yungens!

The most modern coffeeshop in the city! Completely remodelled with white marble with a modern apothecary / drugstore theme!

Boerejongens took over from old coffeeshop Get A Life a few years ago. This place is always filled with locals lining up to grab some weed "to go". Very modern interior and great weed menu. Lovely canal view of Oud West houseboats.

Boerejongens has a great menu, both bio and hydro, with at least 15-20 popular strains.
