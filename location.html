Baarsjesweg 239 West-Side

Small story

Boerejongens means "Farmer Boys in Dutch. Pronounce it like boor-a-yungens! Boerejongens recently took over the well loved Stix Coffeeshop. The newest edition to the Boerejongens' family aims to be the most modern coffeeshop in the city. Completely remodelled with white marble, and a modern apothecary / drugstore style.
